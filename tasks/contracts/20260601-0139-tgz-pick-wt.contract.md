## Goal

Prevent repo-harness closeout from silently deleting dirty merged linked worktree deltas. `ship-worktrees.sh --cleanup-merged` must stop on dirty merged worktrees unless the operator explicitly discards generated scaffold-only files.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Dirty merged linked worktree detection in `scripts/ship-worktrees.sh` and its template copy.
  - Explicit `--discard-scaffold-only` cleanup path for generated plan/contract/review/notes scaffold.
  - Conservative dirty cleanup messaging in `scripts/contract-worktree.sh` and its template copy.
  - Helper tests and command guidance for the new closeout guard.
- Out of scope:
  - Automatic patch picking, committing, or branch selection for useful source deltas.
  - Treating `_ops` tgz archives as a closeout success artifact.
  - Recovery of previously archived worktrees.
